How it works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

We stripped client approvals down to what actually matters — no bloated project management, no required accounts, no confusion.

01
📤
Upload your design

Drag in any image or PDF. Approvel creates a versioned project and generates a unique shareable link — ready in seconds.

→
02
📍
Client annotates

Your client opens the link — no login required. They click anywhere on the design to drop a comment pin and leave feedback right where it matters.

→
03
✅
One-click approval

When they're happy, they hit Approve. You get notified instantly. The approval is logged, timestamped, and saved to your project history forever.

Features

Everything your agency needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built for the real workflow of a creative agency — not the imaginary one in a product manager's slide deck.

🔗
Shareable review links

Every version gets a unique URL. Send it in any channel — email, Slack, WhatsApp. Your client just clicks and reviews. No password, no account, no friction.

📍
Pin-point annotations

Comments are anchored to exact coordinates on the design. No more "the button on the left... no the other left." Everyone sees exactly what's being discussed.

🕓
Full version history

Every upload creates a new version. See all rounds of feedback and approvals in one place. Never lose track of what changed, when, and who approved it.

🏷️
White-label links

On Agency plan, your approval links read as your brand — not ours. Clients see a clean, professional experience that reflects your studio, not a third-party tool.

🔔
Instant notifications

Get notified the moment a client leaves a comment or approves. Via email or Slack — your choice. Stop checking in manually and start doing real work.

📄
PDF & image support

Upload JPG, PNG, PDF — whatever format you deliver in. Each page of a PDF is individually reviewable with its own annotation layer. Built for real deliverables.
